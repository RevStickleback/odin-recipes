Beans and egg

Description

This dish is simply the best. Who needs foie gras when you can have homegrown bread with salt-of-the-Earth beans, topped with a delicious fried egg

Highly recommended.

Ingredients

  • Bread (toasted)
  • Baked beans
  • Egg (free range, obviously)

Steps

  1. Toast bread
  2. Heat beans in saucepan
  3. Add splash of oil to frying pan and, when hot, crack egg
  4. Meanwhile, butter toast
  5. When beans are hot, pour over toast, and then place fried egg on top
  6. Season to taste
